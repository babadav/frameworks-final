Free Amenities

Savor time-tested diner favorites and share good times at 1Bite2Go Café & Deli in Xinyi, the Manhattan of Taipei. Located in the center of the Xinyi commercial and shopping district, the contemporary diner is right across from Taipei 101 and Taipei World Trade Center Hall 1.

From all day brunch to social gatherings to post-clubbing pick me up, there’s an authentic deli classic for every taste. During afternoon tea, re-energize with a tempting American pie and gourmet coffee in a relaxed setting.

Step into the diner and feel immediately the vibe of The Big Apple: fun, outgoing staff, colorful fixed-gear bikes hanging high above in the center, and a large collection of photos depicting the city’s street scenes from urban bikers to fresh produce in farmers’ market.

There is also a 150-inch TV screen for your enjoyment of music programs and major sports events.

Planning a working lunch, an office party, or a family gathering? The private dining room can cater up to 12 guests. Alternatively, try 1Bite2Go Xinyi’s convenient delivery service.

Delivery Service

Delivery service is available for valid addresses within 500 meters of 1Bite2Go Xinyi from 11:00 am to 9:00 pm daily.

Delivery service is available for any order of TWD1,000 or above from the 1Bite2Go Xinyi menu. All prices are subject to 10% service charge.

To ensure strict quality assurance standards, certain food items such as ice cream are not available for delivery.

Payment must be made by cash at point of delivery. No credit card payment will be accepted. There is no refund, change, or cancellation once the order has been confirmed.

To ensure the safety of our riders, 1Bite2Go delivery may be temporarily unavailable, due to bad weather, traffic conditions or other unforeseen circumstances.

Cannot be used in conjunction with any promotion.
